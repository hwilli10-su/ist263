Jodi Picoult demonstrated her range as a writer in her book Small Great Things. The ability to write about a topic outside of her own experience and put herself in someone else's shoes is a hard thing to do well, but she managed to pull it off masterfully. A gut wrenching page turner I would highly recommend to anyone trying to understand race relations and the intracacies of interpersonal relationships.
