Welcome to Lit Swap!

Lit Swap is a platform that provides book lovers the opportunity to become members of a like minded book trading community! All registered books are from other members of this community, and each book will come with annotations from its previous reader to enhance the readers experience and make their reading a further engaging process.

The Process


Select whether you are reading for fun or academia
Choose the book you are looking for from that category
Read reviews to ensure it is what you are looking for
Submit a form to have it delivered to you
Leave a review and discuss the book with others after you read
